Luke Bailey

I am a first year Computer Science PhD Student at Stanford University. I am supported by a Stanford Graduate Fellowship and Future of Life Institute Vitalik Buterin Fellowship.

Previously, I completed a joint degree in Computer Science and Mathematics at Harvard Universty. During my undergraduate degree, I conducted research with the Harvard DtAK lab, advised by Weiwei Pan, Yaniv Yacoby, and Siddharth Swaroop. I also conducted research at the UC Berkeley Center for Human Compatible AI, advised by Scott Emmons.

I am interested in making AI models safer and more robust. If you are interested in collaborating, please reach out.
